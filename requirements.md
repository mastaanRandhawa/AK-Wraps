# Website Requirements Document (WRD)

# AK Wraps & Customs

## Overview

AK Wraps & Customs is a vehicle customization and detailing company serving Surrey, Delta, and the Greater Vancouver area. The website should showcase services, display previous work, build customer trust, and encourage visitors to contact the business or request a quote.

The website should present AK Wraps & Customs as a premium automotive customization shop specializing in vinyl wraps, paint protection, detailing, and vehicle enhancements.
